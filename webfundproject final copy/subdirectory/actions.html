Actions That You Can Take

1. Spreading Awareness:
Although the elephant population is dwindling with each passing day, there are still many people that do not know the full extent of the situation. Thus, we can all do our part by signing on an online form by the World Wildlife Fund (WWF) to spread awareness of the elephant population. Furthermore, we can share this page on social media such as Facebook or Twitter to further spread awareness of the elephant population.

2. Donating to help the cause:
As most companies like WWF are a non-profit organization and a non-governmental organization, we can donate to help the cause. We can donate to help the elephant population by donating to save the elephants.
